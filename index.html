The Experience

Succulent Driftwood
Garden Workshops

Step into a sun-dappled creative space where driftwood, succulents and coastal calm come together. You'll design and build a living sculpture — a one-of-a-kind piece of Byron Bay to take home.

Choose your own unique piece of Byron Bay to take home with you.

Whether you're a total beginner or a seasoned maker, Linda guides you through every step with warmth, expertise and a genuine love for the craft. It's meditative, joyful, and deeply satisfying.
